Time Is Fluid

In physical theater, time flows forward. In Virtual Theater, time is a dimension we sculpt. We can loop it, reverse it, or twist it into a circle.

THE SOUNDSCAPE

We build on foundations of sound. 3-hour ambient sets that dictate the architecture. The environment listens. It learns. A virtual gallery notices where you whisper and dim the lights. The space evolves through use.

THE LOOP

Like a snake eating its own tail, our narratives (especially in "Hatch") explore Circular Time. A performance can be midnight in New York and noon in Tokyo simultaneously. The perfect hour is whenever you arrive.
